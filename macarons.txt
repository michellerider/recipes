Macarons for 12 people

Ingredients:
- 30g egg white (3 to 5 days old)
- 50g grounded almonds
- 50g powdered sugar
- 13g sugar

Step-by-Step instructions
1. Mix grounded almonds with powdered sugar.
2. Mix the egg white until it's begins to stiffen up, then add the sugar.
3. Fold almonds-powdered sugar mix under the egg white. 
4. Fill mixture into piping bag and form the macarons.
5. Leave the mixture for ad least 20 to 60 minutes until the shell hardens.
6. Bake the macarons for 18 minutes in the oven (145Â°C).
